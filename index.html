About Me

I'm currently a 4th-year PhD student in Computer Science at Rice University. My advisor is Prof. T. S. Eugene Ng and I work closely with Prof. Ang Chen. My research interest generally lies in the modeling, designing and implementation of networking systems. In particular, I work on problems that revolve around the performance, fault-tolerance, and reliability issues that arise within modern datacenters. I'm interested in exploiting emerging underlying technologies such as optical devices and programmable switching ASICs to solve those problems.

I obtained M.S. degree from Nanjing University and B.S. degree from Wuhan University, both in Computer Science.
